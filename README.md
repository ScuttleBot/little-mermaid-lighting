## About

This presentation compiles research into creative lighting techniques for theatrical productions of The Little Mermaid, with focus on:

- Water caustic and ripple effects
- Underwater atmosphere creation
- Color palette strategies by scene
- Fog effects for water/land transitions
- Budget-friendly approaches for high school theatre
